3. **SPA admin** (most complex, binding)

## 4. Correct Deal Flow

**NCNDA → KYC → IMFPA → SPA**

**Proper business sequence:**
```
1. LOI/Request Stage
   - Generate and share LOI
   - Establish initial terms and interest
   
2. Compliance & Framework Stage
   - NCNDA (confidentiality & non-circumvention)
   - KYC (counterparty verification)
   - IMFPA (payment terms & master framework)

3. Commercial Offer Stage
   - Receive and review FCO from seller
   - Commercial negotiation
   
4. Final Binding Stage
   - Generate and execute SPA
   - Payment and delivery
```

**Key insight:** LOI is the request stage. FCO is received from the seller (not generated by system). SPA is the final binding document.

## 6. Hard Safety Rule

🚨 **CRITICAL: Never Send Documents Externally Without Approval**

The system MUST:
- ❌ NEVER send documents to buyers/sellers without explicit CEO/Dean approval
- ❌ NEVER send via email, WhatsApp, or any external channel automatically
- ❌ NEVER bypass human review before external communication

**Why:** All documents represent PentraCore's legal and commercial positions. Every external communication requires human decision-making and approval.

## 7. Recommended Next Build Step

### Deal Readiness / Missing-Documents Checklist

**Purpose:** Help CEO and Dean understand deal progress and identify next actions.

**Answers these questions:**
- What documents have been completed/signed?
- What is missing before the next stage?
- Is the deal ready to move from LOI/request → FCO review → SPA?
- What are the immediate action items?

**Example output:**
```
Deal: Copper Cathode (ID: 123)
Current Stage: IMFPA_SIGNED

✅ COMPLETED
  • NCNDA (signed, 2026-05-03)
  • KYC (signed, 2026-05-04)
  • IMFPA (signed, 2026-05-05)

⏳ AWAITING
  • FCO from seller (expected by 2026-05-10)

📋 NEXT ACTIONS
  1. Follow up with seller for FCO
  2. Review FCO upon receipt
  3. Generate and prepare SPA
  4. Schedule execution meeting

🚀 READY TO MOVE TO SPA?
  ✗ No - awaiting FCO from seller
  
Timeline: Once FCO received → 2-3 days for SPA prep → execution
```

**Scope:**
- List all completed documents and signatures
- Identify missing required documents
- Show deal readiness status
- Suggest action items
- Provide timeline guidance

## 8. What This System Is NOT (Yet)

⏸️ **Future capabilities, not in current build:**

- **FCO Generation:** System receives FCO from seller, doesn't generate it
- **Document Signing:** Currently marks as "signed" in status, not actual e-signature
- **External Sharing:** All document sharing is manual (download + email/WhatsApp)
- **Automatic Notifications:** No automated reminders or alerts
- **Document Compiler:** Future feature to ingest historical documents and extract data
